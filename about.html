About BitPadLabs

Our mission, values, and approach

Our Mission

BitPadLabs is built around a powerful mission: help real people solve real problems through clean, scalable technology, and teach the next generation how to do the same.

Our Story

BitPadLabs is a lean, modern business founded by Christopher Inman (Founding Member & CTO), Hannah Cassels, and Jaime Inman. As of 2025, Hannah and Jaime are 50/50 owners, with Chris serving as a founding member and CTO. We are now proudly operating in both Georgia and Mississippi, building useful software that makes a difference without the startup hype.

Our Approach

We believe in human-centered design, privacy-first development, and sustainable business practices. Rather than chasing venture capital or quick exits, we're building products that stand the test of time and help people in their everyday lives.

Core Principles

  • Strategic Vision over Short-Term Gains — We think in decades and execute in sprints.
  • Brand and Culture First — Our code is our craftsmanship, our UI is our storefront.
  • Solve Real Problems with Tech — No vanity projects. No tech theater.
  • Lead with Data, Design with Empathy — We build with users, not just for them.
  • Global-Ready, Local-Savvy — All BitPad products are born globally competitive.

Our Values

Delight

We strive to create experiences that bring joy and satisfaction.

Trust

Transparency and reliability are at the core of everything we build.

Precision

We believe in attention to detail and thoughtful craftsmanship.

Play

Innovation thrives in an environment of experimentation and fun.
