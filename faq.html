Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about BitPadLabs and our products

About BitPadLabs

What is BitPadLabs?

+

BitPadLabs is an independent software development lab founded in Mississippi. We focus on creating clean, scalable software solutions that solve real problems for real people. Our company was founded by Christopher Inman and Hannah Cassels with a mission to blend technology with human-centered design.

Where is BitPadLabs located?

+

BitPadLabs, LLC is a Mississippi-based company. While we operate primarily as a remote team, our company is legally registered in Mississippi.

What does your company name mean?

+

The name "BitPadLabs" combines "Bit" (representing digital technology) and "Pad" (a comfortable space), creating the concept of a comfortable digital workspace where technology thrives. The "Labs" portion emphasizes our commitment to innovation and experimentation.

Our Products

What is budsyapp?

+

budsyapp is a privacy-focused cannabis unit tracking app designed for medical cannabis patients. It helps users track their monthly allotment limits as defined by their state regulations. Initially focusing on Mississippi's medical program, budsyapp plans to expand to support multiple states.

What is GlowStatus?

+

GlowStatus is a smart light integration tool that syncs your Google Calendar with Govee smart lights to visually display your availability status. It's perfect for remote workers, streamers, and busy households who need visual indicators of when someone is in a meeting or needs focus time.

Are your products open source?

+

GlowStatus is available as an open-source project under the MIT license. You can find the source code on our GitHub. budsyapp has some open-source components, but the core application is proprietary. We believe in transparency and community-driven development where possible.

Collaboration & Support

How can I contribute to your projects?

+

We welcome contributions! The best way to contribute is through our GitHub repositories. You can submit issues, pull requests, or join discussions. For GlowStatus specifically, join our Discord server where we coordinate community contributions.

How can I get support for your products?

+

Support is available through several channels: GitHub Discussions for technical issues, product-specific Discord servers for community support, or direct email to info@bitpadlabs.com for business inquiries. Each product also has dedicated documentation available on its respective website.

Do you offer custom software development services?

+

While our primary focus is on our own products, we selectively take on custom development projects that align with our expertise and mission. If you have a project in mind, please contact us at info@bitpadlabs.com with details, and we'll let you know if it's a good fit.
